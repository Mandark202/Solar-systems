
# 🌌 Solar System Explorer Website

## 📖 Project Overview

The Solar System Explorer is an interactive educational website designed to provide users with detailed information about the Sun, planets, moons, asteroids, and other celestial bodies in our solar system. The website aims to make astronomy learning engaging through attractive visuals, animations, and easy-to-understand content.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Educate users about the Solar System.
* Provide detailed information about each planet.
* Display planetary characteristics such as size, mass, temperature, and distance from the Sun.
* Create an interactive and visually appealing user experience.
* Promote interest in space science and astronomy.

---

## 🌎 Planet Information Included

### Mercury

* Closest planet to the Sun.
* Smallest planet in the Solar System.

### Venus

* Hottest planet due to greenhouse effect.
* Similar size to Earth.

### Earth

* Only known planet supporting life.
* 71% covered by water.

### Mars

* Known as the Red Planet.
* Target for future human exploration.

### Jupiter

* Largest planet in the Solar System.
* Has the Great Red Spot.

### Saturn

* Famous for its ring system.
* Gas giant planet.

### Uranus

* Rotates on its side.
* Ice giant planet.

### Neptune

* Farthest known planet from the Sun.
* Strongest winds in the Solar System.
